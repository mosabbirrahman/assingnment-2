Change Your Habits

Changing habits can be challenging but definitely doable with some strategies.

Movement

Incorporate movement into your daily schedule. Consistency is key to forming a habit, so aim to exercise at the same time each day if possible.

Time

By implementing these strategies, you can gradually improve how you manage your time and develop more effective habits that support your goals and priorities.

Practice

Establish a regular practice routine that fits into your daily or weekly schedule. Consistency is key to making progress and forming habits.

Weight loss

Define a realistic and achievable weight loss goal based on your current health status and body composition. Aim for steady progress rather than rapid weight loss.
